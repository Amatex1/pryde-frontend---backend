Respecting Others

Pronouns and Identity

Always respect people's:

  • Pronouns: Use the pronouns people share in their profiles
  • Names: Use people's chosen names and display names
  • Identity: Don't question, invalidate, or mock someone's gender identity, sexual orientation, or relationship status

Boundaries

  • Respect when someone says "no" or asks you to stop contacting them
  • Don't share someone else's private information or content without permission
  • Ask before tagging people in posts or photos
  • Respect content warnings and trigger warnings

Disagreements

It's okay to disagree, but do it respectfully:

  • Focus on ideas, not personal attacks
  • Listen to understand, not just to respond
  • Know when to disengage from unproductive conversations
  • Report serious violations instead of engaging in arguments

Content Guidelines

What You Can Share

  • Personal photos and selfies (non-explicit)
  • Artwork, creative projects, and photography
  • Personal stories and experiences
  • Thoughts, opinions, and discussions
  • Resources and helpful information
  • Event announcements and community organizing

Copyright and Ownership

  • Only post content you own or have permission to share
  • Give credit to original creators when sharing others' work
  • Don't use copyrighted music, images, or videos without permission
  • Respect intellectual property rights

Sensitive Content

If you're sharing content that might be sensitive or triggering:

  • Use content warnings (CW) or trigger warnings (TW)
  • Be specific about what the warning is for
  • Consider whether the content is appropriate for Pryde

Reporting and Enforcement

How to Report

If you see content or behavior that violates these guidelines:

  • Use the "Report" button on posts, profiles, or messages
  • Email support@prydeapp.com with subject "Safety Report" for urgent safety concerns
  • Provide as much detail as possible to help us investigate

All reports are reviewed confidentially. We take every report seriously.

What Happens After a Report

  1. Review: Our safety team reviews the report and investigates
  2. Decision: We determine if guidelines were violated
  3. Action: We take appropriate enforcement action
  4. Follow-up: We may contact you for more information or to update you

Enforcement Actions

Depending on the severity and frequency of violations, we may:

  • Warning: Send a warning for first-time or minor violations
  • Content Removal: Remove violating posts, photos, or comments
  • Temporary Suspension: Suspend your account for a set period
  • Permanent Ban: Permanently ban your account for serious or repeated violations
  • Law Enforcement Referral: Report illegal activity to authorities

Appeals

If you believe we made a mistake:

  • You can appeal enforcement decisions
  • Email support@prydeapp.com with subject "Appeal" and include your appeal details
  • Include your username and details about the decision
  • We'll review your appeal and respond within 7 business days

Your Responsibility

As a member of the Pryde community, you're responsible for:

  • Reading and following these guidelines
  • Treating others with respect and kindness
  • Reporting violations when you see them
  • Being part of the solution, not the problem
  • Helping create a safe, inclusive space for everyone
Remember: Pryde is what we make it together. Your actions shape the community. Choose kindness, choose respect, choose to make Pryde a better place for everyone.
