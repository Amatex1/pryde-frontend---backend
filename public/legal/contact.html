What to Include in Your Email

To help us assist you faster, please include:

  • Your username (if applicable)
  • Email address associated with your account
  • Detailed description of your issue or question
  • Screenshots (if relevant and safe to share)
  • Steps to reproduce (for technical issues)
  • Any error messages you received

Response Times

We aim to respond to all inquiries as quickly as possible:

  • Urgent safety issues: 1-4 hours
  • Safety reports: 24-48 hours
  • General support: 24-72 hours
  • Privacy/Legal requests: 7 business days

Response times may be longer during weekends, holidays, or high-volume periods. We appreciate your patience!
