1. Our Safety Commitment

Pryde is committed to:

  • Zero tolerance for harassment, hate speech, and harmful content
  • Swift action on reports of safety violations
  • Confidential investigations that protect reporter privacy
  • Fair enforcement based on evidence and context
  • Transparency about our processes and decisions
  • Continuous improvement of our safety systems

2. How to Report

2.1 Reporting Content or Behavior

You can report violations in several ways:

üì± In-App Reporting (Fastest)

  • Click the "Report" button on any post, comment, or profile
  • Select the type of violation
  • Provide additional details (optional but helpful)
  • Submit the report

üìß Email Reporting

For urgent safety concerns or detailed reports:

  • Safety Team: safety@prydeapp.com
  • Include: username, description of violation, links/screenshots if available
  • We respond to urgent reports within 24 hours

2.2 What to Include in a Report

To help us investigate effectively, please include:

  • Username of the person or account involved
  • Description of what happened and why it's concerning
  • Links to specific posts, comments, or messages
  • Screenshots (if applicable and safe to capture)
  • Date and time when the incident occurred
  • Context that might help us understand the situation

2.3 Anonymous Reporting

You can report violations anonymously:

  • Your identity will not be shared with the reported user
  • We may not be able to follow up with you if we need more information
  • Anonymous reports are still investigated thoroughly

4. Our Investigation Process

4.1 How We Review Reports

  1. Receipt: We receive and log your report
  2. Triage: We prioritize based on severity and urgency
  3. Investigation: We review the content, context, and any evidence
  4. Decision: We determine if guidelines were violated
  5. Action: We take appropriate enforcement action
  6. Follow-up: We may contact you for more information or updates

4.2 Investigation Timeline

  • Urgent safety threats: Reviewed within 1-4 hours
  • Serious violations: Reviewed within 24 hours
  • Standard reports: Reviewed within 48-72 hours
  • Complex cases: May take up to 7 days

4.3 Confidentiality

All investigations are confidential:

  • Your report is only seen by authorized safety team members
  • We don't share reporter identities with reported users
  • Investigation details are kept private
  • We may access private messages only when necessary for safety investigations

6. Appeals Process

6.1 How to Appeal

If you believe an enforcement decision was made in error:

  1. Email legal@prydeapp.com
  2. Include your username and details about the decision
  3. Explain why you believe the decision was incorrect
  4. Provide any additional context or evidence

6.2 Appeal Review

  • Appeals are reviewed by a different team member than the original decision-maker
  • We respond to appeals within 7 business days
  • Decisions may be upheld, modified, or reversed
  • Appeal decisions are final

7. Protecting Yourself

7.1 Safety Tips

  • Privacy settings: Review and adjust who can see your posts and profile
  • Block users: Block anyone who makes you uncomfortable
  • Don't share personal info: Avoid sharing your address, phone number, or financial details
  • Trust your instincts: If something feels wrong, it probably is
  • Report early: Don't wait for situations to escalate

7.2 Blocking Users

You can block any user at any time:

  • Blocked users cannot see your posts or profile
  • They cannot message you or interact with your content
  • Blocking is private‚Äîthey won't be notified
  • You can unblock users at any time

7.3 Managing Messages

  • You can control who can message you in your privacy settings
  • You can delete conversations at any time
  • Report inappropriate messages before deleting (for investigation)
