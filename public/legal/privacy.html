Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 24, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Pryde collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information. We're committed to transparency and giving you control over your data.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

When you create an account and use Pryde, you provide us with:

  • Account Information: Username, email address, password (encrypted)
  • Profile Information: Display name, bio, pronouns, gender identity, relationship status, location, website
  • Content: Posts, photos, images, comments, messages
  • Communications: Messages you send to other users or to our support team

1.2 Information We Collect Automatically

When you use Pryde, we automatically collect:

  • Usage Data: Pages viewed, features used, time spent on platform
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, IP address
  • Log Data: Access times, error logs, performance data

1.3 Information from Others

We may receive information about you from other users, such as:

  • When someone tags you in a post or photo
  • When someone reports content you've posted
  • When someone adds you as a friend

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

Purpose Details
Provide the Service Create and maintain your account, display your profile, enable messaging and posting
Improve Pryde Analyze usage patterns, fix bugs, develop new features
Keep You Safe Detect and prevent abuse, spam, harassment, and illegal activity
Communicate Send you service updates, security alerts, and respond to your inquiries
Comply with Law Meet legal obligations, respond to legal requests, enforce our Terms

3. How We Store Your Information

3.1 Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using:

  • Database: MongoDB for user accounts, profiles, and posts
  • File Storage: GridFS for photos and images
  • Encryption: Messages are encrypted and stored securely
  • Image Processing: Images are optimized and compressed for performance

3.2 Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted passwords using bcrypt
  • Secure HTTPS connections
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and authentication
Note: While we take security seriously, no system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but we continuously work to protect your data.

4. How We Share Your Information

4.1 With Other Users

Your profile information and posts are visible to other users based on your privacy settings. This includes:

  • Your username, display name, profile photo, and bio
  • Posts and content you share publicly
  • Your friends list (if you choose to make it visible)

4.2 With Service Providers

We may share data with trusted service providers who help us operate Pryde, such as:

  • Cloud hosting providers
  • Analytics services
  • Email service providers

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for the services they provide to us.

4.3 For Legal Reasons

We may disclose your information if required by law or if we believe it's necessary to:

  • Comply with legal obligations or court orders
  • Protect the rights, property, or safety of Pryde, our users, or the public
  • Investigate or prevent illegal activity, fraud, or security threats
  • Respond to law enforcement requests

4.4 We Do NOT Sell Your Data

We will never sell your personal information to third parties. Your data is not a product.

5. Your Privacy Rights

5.1 Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Update: Edit your profile and account information at any time
  • Delete: Delete your account and associated data
  • Export: Download your data in a portable format
  • Object: Object to certain data processing activities

5.2 How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, you can:

  • Use your account settings to update or delete information
  • Contact us at support@prydeapp.com (subject: "Privacy Request")
  • Submit a formal request through our Contact Page

5.3 Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account:

  • Most data is deleted immediately
  • Some data may be retained for legal or safety reasons (e.g., records of banned accounts)
  • Backup copies may persist for up to 90 days

6. Messages and Communications

6.1 Private Messages

Your private messages are:

  • Encrypted and stored securely
  • Accessible only to you and the recipients
  • Not read by Pryde staff under normal circumstances

6.2 When We May Access Messages

We may access encrypted messages only in limited circumstances:

  • When required by law or court order
  • To investigate reports of illegal activity or serious safety threats
  • To prevent imminent harm to users or others

Any access is logged, reviewed, and conducted only by authorized personnel.

7. Cookies and Tracking

7.1 What We Use

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Keep you logged in
  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze how you use Pryde
  • Improve performance and user experience

7.2 Your Choices

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect your ability to use certain features of Pryde.

8. International Users

Pryde is operated from Australia but available worldwide. If you access Pryde from outside Australia:

  • Your data may be transferred to and stored in Australia
  • We comply with Australian privacy laws and standards
  • We respect international privacy rights where applicable

9. Children's Privacy

Pryde is only for users 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18. If we discover that a user is under 18, we will delete their account immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will:

  • Notify you via email
  • Post a notice on the platform
  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page

Continued use of Pryde after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

11. Contact Us About Privacy

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding your privacy or this policy:

  • Email: support@prydeapp.com
  • Contact Form: Contact Page

For privacy-specific requests, please include "Privacy Request" in your email subject line.
