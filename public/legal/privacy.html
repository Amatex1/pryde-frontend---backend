Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 24, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Pryde collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information. We're committed to transparency and giving you control over your data.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

When you create an account and use Pryde, you provide us with:

  • Account Information: Username, email address, password (encrypted)
  • Profile Information: Display name, bio, pronouns, gender identity, relationship status, location, website
  • Content: Posts, photos, images, comments, messages
  • Communications: Messages you send to other users or to our support team

1.2 Information We Collect Automatically

When you use Pryde, we automatically collect:

  • Usage Data: Pages viewed, features used, time spent on platform
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, IP address
  • Log Data: Access times, error logs, performance data

1.3 Information from Others

We may receive information about you from other users, such as:

  • When someone tags you in a post or photo
  • When someone reports content you've posted
  • When someone adds you as a friend

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

Purpose Details
Provide the Service Create and maintain your account, display your profile, enable messaging and posting
Improve Pryde Analyze usage patterns, fix bugs, develop new features
